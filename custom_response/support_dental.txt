**1. Employer support**

Under Ministry of Manpower regulations, if dental treatment is deemed necessary for health by a Singapore-registered medical or dental professional, employers have to bear the cost of dental treatment, regardless of whether the condition is work-related or not. (More information [here](https://www.mom.gov.sg/faq/work-permit-for-foreign-worker/are-employers-responsible-for-the-cost-of-a-work-permit-or-s-pass-holders-dental-treatment))

**2. HealthServe**

HealthServe provides dental consultation and medication for a flat fee of $15. Fees will be waived for migrant workers who are Special Pass holders or have been referred by other partner NGOs. (More information [here](https://www.healthserve.org.sg/medical-dental-services/))

You may contact Healthserve and seek dental support at:
Phone: +65 3129 5000 (24-hour helpline)
Address: 1 Lorong 23 Geylang, #01-07, Building 4, Singapore 388352
Clinic hours: 6:30pm to 9:30pm every Tuesdays, Wednesdays & Thursdays (closed on public holidays)

**3. HOME**

HOME offers subsidised dental services and outpatient treatment for our shelter residents.
